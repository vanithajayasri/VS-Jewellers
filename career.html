VS Jewellers is a jewellery store.
Choosing a career is the most important part of any job search. We have approximately two job profiles which you can use to help you make an informed career choice and gain a better understanding of what's involved in the job.

Two categories of jobs are avaliable.
  1. Jewellery Designer.
  2. Jewellery Sales Clerk.
Minimum Job qualifications:
  1. Willingness to approach customers & offer assistance.
  2. Safely operate power equipment.
  3. Assemble furniture.
  4. Flexible schedule to accommodate day, night & weekend shifts.
  5. High School diploma or equivalent.
  6. Exceptional customer service skills.
  7. Excellent listening and oral communication skills.
  8. Holding consultations with commissioning clients.
