PROFILE ABOUT JEWELLERY SALES CLERK.

Jewellery designers design and often make jewellery using a variety of materials, including gold, silver and precious stones.

    List of Jewelry sales clerk duties:
  1. Promote sales by assisting customers and offering suggestions.
  2. Ability to communicate professionally in person and by phone.
  3. Provide prompt and courteous service to all customers.
  4. Utilize product knowledge to promote sales.
  5. Responsible for ensuring store appearance is clean and presentable at all times.
  6. Enter register transactions accurately.
